Brunch Spot of the month

Cafe Miami - Instagram Paradise

Café Miami is a part of The Strand Building, E9 – a beautiful art deco building with bright, sunny decor (and lots of Millennial Pink). The food also combines traditional American with touches of Mexican, Spanish and Cuban. Try the (heart shaped) waffle burger with chorizo, avocado and bright orange hot sauce - simply delicious!
